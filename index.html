About The Office

"The Office" is a hilarious sitcom that captures the everyday lives of the employees at the Dunder Mifflin paper company. Follow the antics of Michael Scott, Jim Halpert, Pam Beesly, and the rest of the gang as they navigate the quirky world of office politics.
